A food delivery service has to deal with lot of
perishable raw materials which makes it all.
The most important factor for such a company
is to accurately forecast daily and weekly demand.
It is based upon the real-time analysis of demand
which was there in the past for that particular product
or service in the market present today.
Demand forecasting must be done by a scientific
approach and facts, events which are related to the forecasting must be considered.
